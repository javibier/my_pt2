About Us

We are here to help you move. And, not just move, but move with energy and intention, experiencing everything life has to offer. Regardless of injury, fitness or age, your interaction with the world around you depends on your ability to move, and the quality of your interaction depends on your willingness to maximize your own physical potential. At Yosemite Physical Therapy, we work to improve your physical health and quality of life through science-based therapies delivered in a professional and compassionate atmosphere. We will make you a partner in your own recovery by teaching you to make good health decisions, maintain optimum physical function and reduce your chances of re-injury.
